## About Me

Hand me a business problem and I'll build the technology that solves it.

- I lead **digital transformation projects** — BI platforms, process automation, and AI-powered tools that change how companies operate.
- I've worked across **healthcare, oil & gas, manufacturing, real estate, and logistics** — the industry changes; building the right tech doesn't.
- I design **analytics ecosystems** end to end: SQL pipelines on Microsoft Fabric, tabular models with DAX, and executive dashboards in Power BI.
- I build **full-stack software**: Azure-hosted apps backed by SQL databases and serverless functions.
- I create **AI agents, bots, and automation flows** that read documents, execute tasks, and take work off people's plates.
- I care about **clean code**, docs-as-code, and knowledge management with Obsidian & Notion.

> Most of my day-to-day work lives in **private client repositories** — happy to talk shop on [LinkedIn](https://www.linkedin.com/in/gerardodominguezramirez/).
